My name is Seth. I Was raised in upstate NY, but I relocated to San Francisco in 2016, working as the Technology Manager for a design/research lab (the startup wing of a large, lumbering outsourcing firm). 

After almost a year and a half, I left the enterprise world to join the team at [SourceClear](https://www.sourceclear.com/), a startup doing cool things in the Open Source Security world, and it changed my life. I was introduced to containers and Kubernetes, then thrown into the deep end, supporting a fully containerized production environment. I knew immediately I wanted to get more involved in this Cloud Native world. 

After SourceClear's acquisition by CA Technologies in April 2018, I continued my containerization deep dive as part of the [Veracode](https://www.veracode.com/) family, eventually leaving the company in early 2019 as a Senior DevOps Engineer. Since then, I've joined the music app startup [Triller](https://triller.co) as their Site Reliability Engineer, leading their migration from monolith to containers. 

I’ve spent the last few years learning all I can about containers, Kubernetes, and all things cloud, here you’ll find thoughts, musings, maybe tutorials, and other things that I feel worth writing down for others to see.
